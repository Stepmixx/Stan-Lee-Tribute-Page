STAN LEE

"The Man"

Here's a short biography

Stanley Martin Lieber, better known as 'Stan Lee', born on December 28, 1922 in New York City, was an American comic book writer and editor who introduced the world to characters notable for their complexity and realism. He was the co-creator, along with artists like Steve Ditko or Jack Kirby of several superheroes, expanding Marvel Comics, taking it from a small advertising house to a large multimedia corporation. Even today, Marvel comics are distinguished by always indicating 'Stan Lee presents' in the presentation labels.

Lee died on November 12, 2018, six weeks before his 96th birthday at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles, California, after being rushed there in a medical emergency earlier in the day.
